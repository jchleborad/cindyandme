A little about us...

We met in a small class at a small college in a small town. It was April and we had just a short time together before we left for summer break. We fell immensely in love, survived the time away and were married a short time later. We have raised two fantastic children and are now experiencing life in huge way. These are the places we visit and the stories we tell. Enjoy!

March 22, 2018

Another year older it is. This year for my birthday I received a very unique and special gift. Our own website! This last year has been hard, harder than imagined and was filled with experiences I had feared for quite a while. I found out that those fears, when worked through, were not so scary. We found the one thing we had dreaded the most brought about an opportunity to embrace change. Throughout his life my husband has enjoyed just about everything technology. He produced sport college recruit videos for our son, and others. When he didn’t know how something worked, he has learned. He has always pursued new knowledge and has naturally and intentionally embraced technology.

At 52 he embarked on a path to learn coding. Coding is a myriad of languages that all work together to make really cool things happen. Can you imagine trying to learn multiple languages all at once when you have been monolingual your whole life except the one most important phrase in Spanish, “Una cervesa mas.” After nine months of working his job during the day and spending 4-5 hours each night and countless hours each weekend he completed his course work and submitted his final project. I don’t know who was happier he had completed the course work.

So, back to our website…for my birthday I was given “Cindy and Me” a website developed by him to document the adventures of our life as empty-nesters. The meaning of this website runs deep with me. It is a celebration of 12 months of challenges. It is a tribute to a marriage that has survived almost 29 years. It is a prediction of many wonderful years to come. It is a legacy to show that life together is better than life alone. In 8 days we embark on our first adventure that will be documented on “Cindy and Me” and I can’t wait!
